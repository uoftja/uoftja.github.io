What is UTJA?

The University of Toronto Japan Association is a vibrant club that fosters cultural exchange and language learning. Through our weekly language exchange sessions, we provide a welcoming platform for students to enhance their Japanese language skills while building meaningful connections with fellow language enthusiasts. Additionally, our club organizes a variety of engaging events throughout the year, further promoting the appreciation and understanding of Japanese culture within the University of Toronto community.
